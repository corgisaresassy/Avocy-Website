The Avocy Difference

Telehealth Therapy

Avocy's unique telehealth therapy program connects patients with insurance covered, evidence based care up to three times a week. Avocy tailors it's outpatient program to the patient needs and helps ensure patients work with one of our expert addiction focused therapists to receive the best possible care in their recovery journey.

Insurance

Avocy was built with the patients cost in mind. Many patients will have no out of pocket cost.

Avocy's services are covered by all major health insurance providers and include telehealth therapy up to three times a week.

Avocy works closely with your insurance company to keep patient costs low and treatment can often be free for those who've met their deductibles and out-of-pocket max.
